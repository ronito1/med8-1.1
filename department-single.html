Dental Implants

A dental implant is a titanium screw surgically placed into the jawbone to replace missing teeth. It serves as a strong foundation for artificial teeth, providing a natural look and function.



Why choose dental implant over bridges?

Dental implants are typically more durable and preserve natural teeth compared to bridges. Implants fuse with the jawbone, providing a stable base for artificial teeth, while bridges rely on adjacent teeth for support, potentially compromising their structure.

Healing period

The healing period for a dental implant typically takes 3 to 6 months. This is the time it takes for the implant to fuse with the jawbone, a process called osseointegration.
