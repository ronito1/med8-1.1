What is Gum Disease?

Gum disease (also known as periodontal disease) is a bacterial infection caused by plaque buildup around the teeth and gums. If left untreated, it can lead to tooth loss, bone deterioration, and other health complications. Common signs include bleeding gums, bad breath, gum recession, and loose teeth.

Why Choose Delta Dental Care for Gum Treatment?

Comprehensive Evaluation: We use advanced diagnostic tools to assess the severity of your gum condition before crafting a customized treatment plan.
